Our Story

Golden Hearts Foundation, Non-Profit Organization

Golden Hearts Foundation for orphans and children with disabilities, is endowed with good reason and well founded through motives and intentions, deem it expedient and necessary to form the Ministry afore mentioned. We recognize that characteristically common social and economic problems as a common phenomenon amongst all OVC in Busoga region, Jinja. We appreciate that unity is strength both socially and economically. We are determined to dynamically, positively and effectively improve the social and economical status of all Orphans and Children with disabilities (CWDs) in Busoga region and that of Uganda at large, through the implementation and promotion of non- institutionalised care, independence and self-reliance of all CWDs as provided by the objectives of this constitution. The constitution being the supreme law, the Ministry is sine quo non for the implementation and promotion of the said objectives .

Membership

Membership of the GHF shall be open to all Orphans and children with disabilities (CWDs) and their carer givers living in Busoga Region of Uganda. Any person in sympathy with the Objectives of the GHF may apply to become Associate Member and may do so upon such terms and conditions as the GHF may from time to time determine.

Aim of the Organization

To reclaim and safeguard basic rights of CWDs in Busoga Region to enable them live a happy and fulfilled life.

Benefits of the Organization
  • Strengthening capacity of CWDs through psycho-social support, skills training and education to sustain them and live independent lives.
  • To increase access to care, support and referral of CWDs at risk.

Chairperson

Keneth Sekabila

I am Keneth Sekabila founder of Golden Hearts Foundation, I grew up as a total orphan on the street of Jinja.Later on I met kind hearted people from California USA who became my family and sponsored my education. And this was the Landry family. In this community I grew up seeing many abandoned disabled children whose parents believed these children were cursed resulting into single mothers .Many homes of these special needs broke down because of the physical disabilities of such children . And many could shy/ hide and lock these children in houses because they believed this could spread to other families . And as I was growing , this kind of treatment for special needs touched me and because of what Landry family did for me, I decided to pay it forward by finding anyway to help the abandoned disabled children in Jinja community.
